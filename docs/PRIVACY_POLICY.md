# t1dTracker Privacy Policy (Draft)

This is a short privacy summary intended to be hosted publicly (e.g. GitHub Pages or your website).

Data storage
- Health data (insulin entries, carb counts, glucose readings) is stored locally on the device in the app's private storage.
- Preferences are stored in DataStore; sensitive prefs may be encrypted when device keystore is available.

Backups
- Backups created by the app are encrypted locally with a password you provide. The app cannot recover lost backup passwords.

Telemetry
- Telemetry is disabled by default. If you enable telemetry, only anonymous crash/usage data is sent. Health data is redacted and never transmitted.

Sharing
- Backups you create can be shared manually by you (via the system share sheet). The app does not automatically upload or transmit your health data.

Third parties
- The app may optionally include Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics if you enable telemetry; these SDKs are only initialized when telemetry is enabled.

Contact
- Contact: hardrivetech@proton.me
