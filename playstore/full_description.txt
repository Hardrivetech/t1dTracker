t1dTracker helps people with Type 1 diabetes track insulin doses, carbohydrate intake, and glucose readings — all stored locally on your device.

Features:
- Simple insulin-to-carb and correction dose calculator with rounding options.
- Local history of entries with edit and delete.
- Encrypted backups using a password you control (PBKDF2 + AES-GCM).
- Optional DB encryption with SQLCipher on supported devices (keystore-gated).
- Migration and rollback tooling: automatic pre-migration backups and one-tap restore.

Your data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.
